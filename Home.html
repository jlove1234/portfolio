Isle Royale series

In August 2021, a News Tribune photographer, videographer and I took two trips — a weeklong trip and a day trip — to Isle Royale National Park in Lake Superior to report this series. We started with a Grand Portage Band of Lake Superior flag-raising ceremony, which celebrated the band now having a formal consolation role in decision making at the park because of their cultural connection to the island they call "Minong.

We highlighted the unique and challenge ways visitors travel to the island, and the people that ferry them across unforgiving lake.

And finally, we focused on families that could still use cabins their ancestors made before Isle Royale became a national park, but their special arrangements aren't permanent and the future for the cabins and families place on the island is uncertain.
